Goal

It is a NYT Bestseller book cart with options to filter price categorized in 3 segments, and sort by ranking


Usability Principles

Functionalities


  • Filters: By Genre and Price segments
  • Sort: By Ranking of the book
  • Aggregator: Cart that adds the items by its properties and shows total price
  • Design framework used: Material UI

    • Usability


  • A header of the app discribing the book store
  • 12 book items with categories: title, author, and price
  • An "Add" button to add to the cart dynamically
  • A cart that adds new items by title, increases counter of each item, and clears the cart with a "CLEAR" button
